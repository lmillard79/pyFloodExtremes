MGBT: Multiple Grubbs-Beck Test for Low Outliers¶

This notebook demonstrates the detection of low outliers (potentially influential low flows) using the Multiple Grubbs-Beck Test (MGBT), as recommended by ARR 2019.

Artificial Outlier Test¶

To demonstrate the robustness of the test, we add very small flows to the dataset and re-run the test.

# Add 3 artificial low outliers
flows_with_outliers = flows.copy()

Next Steps¶
